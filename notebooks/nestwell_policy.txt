NESTWELL RETURN & SHIPPING POLICY
Last updated: January 1, 2024

SECTION 1 — RETURN ELIGIBILITY

Most items sold by Nestwell can be returned within 30 days of the delivery date,
provided the item is unused, in its original packaging, and accompanied by proof
of purchase (your order confirmation email is sufficient).

The following items are NOT eligible for return under any circumstances:
  • Items marked as Final Sale at the time of purchase
  • Items that have been assembled, installed, or used
  • Perishable goods and consumables (e.g. cleaning supplies, candles)
  • Gift cards and digital products
  • Customized or personalized items

To check whether your specific order is return-eligible, please provide your
order ID to our support team or use the order lookup feature.

SECTION 2 — HOW TO START A RETURN

Step 1: Confirm your order is within the 30-day return window and not a
        Final Sale item.
Step 2: Contact Nestwell Support with your order ID and reason for return.
        You can reach us via the chat on this page, by email at
        support@nestwell.com, or by calling 1-800-NESTWELL (Mon–Fri, 9am–6pm PT).
Step 3: Our team will send you a prepaid return shipping label by email within
        1 business day.
Step 4: Drop the package off at any UPS or USPS location using the label provided.
Step 5: Once we receive and inspect the item (typically 3–5 business days after
        carrier pickup), we will process your refund.

Note: Items returned without an approved return label will not be accepted and
will be shipped back to the sender at their expense.

SECTION 3 — REFUND TIMELINES

Refunds are issued to the original payment method only.

  • Credit / Debit Card: 5–7 business days after the return is processed
  • PayPal: 3–5 business days after the return is processed
  • Nestwell Store Credit: Issued within 1 business day (optional, faster alternative)

You will receive a confirmation email as soon as your refund is initiated.
If you have not received your refund after 10 business days, please contact us
with your order ID so we can investigate.

SECTION 4 — SHIPPING OPTIONS & COSTS

Standard Shipping (5–8 business days)
  • Free on orders over $75
  • $5.99 on orders under $75

Expedited Shipping (2–3 business days)
  • $12.99 flat rate on all orders

Overnight Shipping (next business day, order by 12pm PT)
  • $24.99 flat rate on all orders

Please note:
  • Shipping times are estimates and do not include order processing time
    (typically 1 business day).
  • Nestwell does not currently ship outside the contiguous United States.
  • P.O. Boxes are accepted for Standard Shipping only.
  • Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to a
    Nestwell error (e.g. wrong item shipped, damaged on arrival).

SECTION 5 — DAMAGED OR INCORRECT ITEMS

If your item arrived damaged or you received the wrong item, please contact us
within 7 days of delivery. We will arrange a free return and ship a replacement
or issue a full refund including original shipping costs — whichever you prefer.
Please include a photo of the damaged or incorrect item when you contact us, as
this helps us process your case faster.

SECTION 6 — EXCHANGES

Nestwell does not offer direct exchanges at this time. If you would like a
different item, please initiate a return for the original item and place a new
order. This ensures the fastest possible turnaround and guarantees stock
availability for the replacement item.
